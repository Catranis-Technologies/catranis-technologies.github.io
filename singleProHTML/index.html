About Us

Catranis Technologies was founded with the vision of enabling anyone to turn their excellent ideas into excellent products and services. It's far too often that we come across great ideas that turn into average products, have unintuitive or outright broken features, or never even make it off of paper. We'd like to change that!

Whether you're looking to make a small personal website or a scalable mobile application, Catranis Technologies will make your ideas into a reality.

Android and iOS Development

We build leading edge apps with excellent design and outstanding functionality to make your ideas stand out from the crowd. Our apps are built with effortless user flow and big data scalability in mind, so whether your app has hundreds or millions of users, nothing will get in the way.

Transparent and Informed

We love bright ideas, so we won't leave you in the dark while we're engineering your ideas into real products. Our team does its best to keep you informed and give you realistic estimates and projections for any idea you undertake with us.

Clean Design

Our team follows Apple and Google design guidelines to bring you clean user designs and interfaces so that your users can focus on what they're doing, and not how to do it.

Responsive Websites

We don't want you limited to one screen size or device, so all of our designs are adaptable to look great on all screen sizes. If you'd like a demo, try out this website on your mobile phone, tablet, and computer to see how it adapts.

Planning & Strategy

Not sure where to start? Need some advice and forethought? We can assist you in mapping out the potential technical challenges in developing your ideas. In addition, we can create a strategic roadmap for overcoming these challenges in timely and resourceful fashion.

Technology Consulting

If you already have a software product or service, we are able to offer advice on how well it's running, potential improvements, as well as simply explain the technical aspects of your product or service so you can get a better understanding of what's going on.

Web App & Site Development

We can create an outstanding website or web app for you to integrate with an existing business or run enitrely by itself. Simply having an online presense or adding new functionality will greatly help you meet your business needs.

Mobile App Development

We can turn your ideas into full-fledged mobile applications that run on iOS and Android. Our hybrid development process allows us to quickly and simulataneously develop for both platforms to maximize your exposure while minimizing the cost of development.
